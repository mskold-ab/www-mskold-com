# The Founder
[about us](/about) | [our history](/history) | [our products](/products) 

MSkold AB was founded in 2015, but the story behind it starts a decade earlier —
in the trenches of software development, where a passion for tooling and developer
experience quietly took shape.

### 2005–2010 – Roots in Team Foundation Server

The journey began in 2005 with consulting work on Team Foundation Server, the
earliest incarnation of what would eventually become Azure DevOps. Working
directly with development teams made it easy to see where the friction was —
and hard to resist doing something about it. Those early years produced a
steady stream of small open source utilities, reports, and Visual Studio
extensions, each one solving a real problem for a real team.

### 2010–2014 – Building in the Community
In 2010, things accelerated. Joining the ALM Rangers — Microsoft's community
of practice for Azure DevOps — opened the door to collaborating on guidance
materials that reached thousands of teams worldwide. Contributions included
the Branching Guide, the Test Manager Guide, and a range of practical tools
that shaped how organizations approached application lifecycle management.

These were formative years: building relationships with Microsoft, learning
what large-scale development teams actually needed, and developing a reputation
for shipping things that worked.

By the time MSkold AB was incorporated in 2015, the foundation was already
in place — deep product knowledge, a trusted place in the community, and a
clear sense of the problems worth solving.
