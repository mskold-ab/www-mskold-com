# About Us

## 20 years building tools to make teams excel

MSkold AB is a Swedish software company with one focus: making the everyday work of
development teams smoother. We build extensions for Azure DevOps that fill the small
but frustrating gaps — the missing export, the dashboard widget that almost does what
you need, the report you can never quite get out of the system.

Our tools are used by more than 30,000 organizations worldwide, and we are a certified
**Top Publisher** on the Azure DevOps Marketplace.

## Our story

MSkold AB was founded in 2015, but the experience behind it goes back further. A decade
of consulting, community work, and open source contributions to the Azure DevOps
ecosystem gave us an unusually clear picture of what development teams actually struggle with day to day — and what was missing from the tooling available to them. That history is what shaped everything we build today.

If you want to understand what drives us, read about our
[founder's background](/founder) or explore the full [company history](/history).

## Our philosophy

We believe teams do their best work when tooling gets out of the way. Most of the
problems we solve aren't dramatic — they're the small daily friction points that
silently slow teams down. An export that takes too many steps. A dashboard that can't
surface the one number a manager needs. A report that has to be assembled by hand
every sprint.

We build precisely because we've felt that friction ourselves.

## Strong focus on security & privacy

In today's world, security and privacy should be the first concern. At MSkold AB, we
understand that development teams trust us with sensitive information — code, project
data, and organizational workflows. That's why we've made security and data protection
fundamental to everything we build.

Our extensions are designed with a security and privacy-first architecture: all data
processing occurs within the customer's own browser and Azure DevOps environment.
Customer production data is never transmitted, stored, or processed externally.

Our development practices have a strong security focus, and we apply modern practices
and tools like SAST and third-party package scans.

## Enterprise ready
