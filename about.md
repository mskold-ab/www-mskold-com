# About Us

## We build the tools Azure DevOps forgot to include

MSkold AB is a Swedish software company with one focus: making the everyday work of development teams smoother. We build extensions for Azure DevOps that fill the small but frustrating gaps — the missing export, the dashboard widget that almost does what you need, the report you can never quite get out of the system.

Our tools are used by more than 30,000 organizations worldwide, and we're a certified **Top Publisher** on the Azure DevOps Marketplace.

## Our philosophy

We believe teams do their best work when tooling gets out of the way. Most of the problems we solve aren't dramatic — they're the small daily friction points that silently slow teams down. An export that takes too many steps. A dashboard that can't surface the one number a manager needs. A report that has to be assembled by hand every sprint.

We build precisely because we've felt that friction ourselves.


## Seeing is believing

Don't take our word for it — all our extensions are available with a free 30-day trial.  
You can evaluate them in your own environment, no strings attached, no sales call needed. 
Just install directly from the [Azure DevOps Marketplace](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/publishers/mskold) 
and get started in minutes. If it's a fit, self-service credit card billing makes it easy to continue.

## Strong focus on Security & Privacy

In today's world, security and privacy should be the first concern. At MSkold AB, we understand that development teams trust us with sensitive information—code, project data, and organizational workflows. That's why we've made security and data protection fundamental to everything we build.

Our extensions are designed with a security and privacy-first architecture: all data processing occurs within the customer's own browser and Azure DevOps environment. Customer production data is never transmitted, stored, or processed externally.

Our development practices have a strong security focus and we apply modern practices and tools like SAST and 3rd party package scans.

## Enterprise ready

We meet the requirements of security-conscious organizations. On request, we provide:

- **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)** — covering GDPR obligations and data handling
- **Sub-processor list** — full transparency on any third-party services involved
- **Incident response procedures** — documented processes for security events
- **Security questionnaire support** — we'll work with your procurement or security team directly

Reach us at [info@mskold.com](mailto:info@mskold.com) to request any of these.
