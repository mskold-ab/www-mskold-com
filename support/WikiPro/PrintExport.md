# Printing and Exporting

WikiPRO supports both printing and exporting wiki content to Word documents.

## Overview

You can use WikiPRO to print wiki content or export it to Microsoft Word documents.

Some printing capabilities are available for free, while advanced printing and Word export features require a paid subscription.

## Printing

Printing is supported in WikiPRO.

### Free printing

- Printing a single page is free.

### Paid printing features

- Printing multiple pages is a paid feature.
- Printing subtrees is a paid feature.

## Exporting to Word

Exporting wiki content to Word documents is supported in WikiPRO.

### Paid export features

- Exporting a single page to a Word document is a paid feature.
- Exporting page trees to Word documents is a paid feature.

### Custom Word templates

WikiPRO supports custom Word templates when exporting to Word documents. This makes it possible to adapt exported documents to your organization's preferred layout, branding, or document structure.

## Summary

- ✅ Print single pages for free
- 🔒 Print multiple pages
- 🔒 Print subtrees
- 🔒 Export pages to Word documents
- 🔒 Export page trees to Word documents
- 🔒 Use custom Word templates for Word export
